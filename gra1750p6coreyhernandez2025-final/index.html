Our History

Started back in 1975, We came on the scene with only one mission, to give the taste of southern soul food to those who either don't have the time, don't have the funds or, god forbid, don't have the family to make it with. Ever since then, we've been working to bring delicous and hearty food to our customers and the community at large. And we ain't stopping anytime soon!
